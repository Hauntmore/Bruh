    Tagscript Engine

  1. {user.tag} - Get the tag of the author.
  2. {user.username} - Get the username of the author.
  3. {user.mention} - Mentions the author.
  4. {user.nickname} - Fetch the nickname of the author.
  5. {guild.name} - Fetch the guild name.
  6. {guild.memberCount} - This will find the amount of members in the guild and display it in a readable format.
  7. {message.channel} - The channel name of where the command was ran.
  8. {target.mention} - Find the last person that was mentioned in the message.
